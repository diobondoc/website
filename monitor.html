Why a Monitor Matters

Having a quality gaming monitor can make a gamer's performance better. Monitors are the ones that gamers will always look at, so it is important to have a monitor that is smooth, the right size, and the right hertz to make the gaming experience better.

Top Gaming Monitors of 2025

Budget Gaming Monitor: AOC 24G2

The AOC 24G2 is an excellent budget-friendly option that offers a 144Hz refresh rate and 1ms response time, making it perfect for fast-paced gaming. Reference: RTINGS

Key Features:

  • 24-inch Full HD (1920 x 1080) Display
  • 144Hz Refresh Rate
  • 1ms Response Time
  • FreeSync Support

Mid-Tier Gaming Monitor: Dell S2721DGF

The Dell S2721DGF offers a great balance of performance and features with a 165Hz refresh rate and QHD resolution, making it ideal for gamers looking for quality without breaking the bank. Reference: Tom's Hardware

Key Features:

  • 27-inch QHD (2560 x 1440) Display
  • 165Hz Refresh Rate
  • 1ms Response Time
  • G-SYNC Compatible

High-End Gaming Monitor: ASUS ROG Swift PG32UQX

This premium monitor features a 4K resolution, 144Hz refresh rate, and HDR support, making it perfect for gamers who want the best visuals and performance. Reference: TFTCentral

Key Features:

  • 32-inch 4K UHD (3840 x 2160) Display
  • 144Hz Refresh Rate
  • 1ms Response Time
  • HDR Support with Mini LED Backlight
  • NVIDIA G-SYNC Ultimate
